About Me

My journey in data science

I grew up obsessed with solving problems, whether it meant breaking down a tricky boss fight, strategizing with teammates, or later, figuring out how to make sense of messy data.

Gaming taught me early on how to approach complex problems. Stay curious, adapt quickly, learn from failure, and always find a way to optimize. Those same habits pulled me toward Economics in high school, and later the Data Science side of my Master's program, where I realized I could use those skills to tackle real-world challenges.

Since then, I've been using data science to help businesses operate smarter. Automating client analysis, building predictive models for employee retention, and even designing an NLP pipeline that extracts insights from SEC earnings calls to connect financial sentiment to stock performance. Every project is just a new puzzle to solve, and that's exactly why I love it.

The same mindset that made me a strong teammate in esports and basketball: collaboration, critical thinking, and constant iteration, is the same one I bring to every role and project I take on.

I just finished a personal machine learning project where I built a model to predict professional match outcomes in Counter-Strike 2 (CS2), a popular esports title with over 1 million concurrent players each month.
